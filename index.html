durlum metal ceilings & lighting solutions

Durlum is a world-leading manufacturer of metal ceiling systems. For decades, durlum has combined aesthetic design with acoustic performance and technical precision — delivering solutions tailored to every project type, scale, and specification.

A perfect fit for every space

Whether you are designing a modern office, high-end retail environment, or a complex transportation hub, durlum delivers solutions tailored to your project requirements with uncompromising quality.

An ideal solution for architects

durlum works closely with architects and designers from concept through to installation. Products are manufactured from high-quality steel and aluminium in a wide range of perforation patterns, finishes, and custom colours.
